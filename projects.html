cardano ticketing protocol

On-chain event ticketing on Cardano using CIP-68 updatable NFTs. Resale prices and royalties are enforced via Plutus scripts on transfer, providing strong anti-scalping protection and artist royalties higher than centralized platforms. Features include an off-chain lightweight interactive venue seat visualizer with POV previews, mobile QR gate check-in (offline-capable), artist pages with upcoming events, organizer analytics for revenue and sales, wallet-signed JWT auth via CIP-30 (no seed phrase exposure), and shareable ticket passport for no-wallet access. All core mechanics align with current Cardano CIP-68 and Plutus capabilities; complex enforcement adds tx cost and requires optimized scripts and oracle mitigation.

Aiken · Lucid Evolution · Blockfrost · Supabase · CIP-30 · CIP-68 · IPFS · React· Tailwind
