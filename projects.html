Cardano Ticketing Protocol

On-chain event ticketing built on Cardano. Tickets are CIP-68 NFTs with post-mint updatability — resale prices are enforced on-chain, no scalpers. Includes an interactive venue seat visualizer with POV previews, mobile QR gate check-in, artist pages with upcoming events, and organizer analytics for revenue, royalties, and sales. Wallet-signed JWT auth via CIP-30 challenge-response (no seed phrase). Shareable ticket passport — works on any device, no crypto wallet needed.

Aiken · Lucid Evolution · Blockfrost · Supabase · CIP-30 · CIP-68 · IPFS · React 18 · Tailwind v4
