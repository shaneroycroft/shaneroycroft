editor guide

composition
crop drag to draw a crop region. choose a preset aspect ratio or lock the current one. apply or cancel with the buttons.
transform rotate 90° cw/ccw, flip horizontal or vertical. straighten corrects a tilted horizon — a grid overlay appears to guide alignment. perspective correction lets you drag four corner handles to fix converging lines. commit bakes the transform in and auto-crops black corners.
composition grid overlay a rule of thirds, golden ratio, diagonal, or 4×4 grid on the canvas to guide composition.
tone & colour
presets apply a built-in film look (kodak portra, fuji velvia, tri-x b&w, cinematic, golden hour, matte fade) or save and recall your own.
adjust exposure, contrast, highlights, shadows, fade, vibrance, saturation, temperature, tint, clarity, sharpening, noise reduction, and grain. click a value to type it directly.
tone curve click on the curve to add control points; drag them to reshape tone. right-click or drag off-canvas to remove a point. applied after all other adjustments.
hsl per-colour-band hue, saturation, and luminance across 8 bands: red, orange, yellow, green, aqua, blue, purple, magenta.
split tone tint shadows and highlights independently. the balance slider shifts which tones are affected.
finishing
vignette darkens corners. amount controls strength; feather controls the blend radius.
border adds a solid-colour border around the image. set width and pick a colour.
label stamp text onto the image in permanent marker. adjust size, opacity, position, and colour. useful for watermarks or captions.
local adjustments
dodge & burn paint exposure directly onto the image. dodge brightens; burn darkens. adjust brush size and strength. use reset to clear the mask.
output
format png (lossless) or jpeg. for jpeg, a quality slider appears (60–100).
before / after hold the button to compare against the original image.
